Male Artist

  • Leonardo da Vinci – Renaissance artist, known for Mona Lisa and The Last Supper.
  • Vincent van Gogh – Post-Impressionist painter, famous for Starry Night and Sunflowers.
  • Pablo Picasso – Co-founder of Cubism, known for Guernica and Les Demoiselles d'Avignon.
  • Salvador Dalí – Surrealist known for The Persistence of Memory.

Female Artists

  • Frida Kahlo – Mexican painter celebrated for her self-portraits and exploration of identity..
  • Georgia O'Keeffe – Known as the "Mother of American Modernism," famous for her paintings of flowers and landscapes.
  • Artemisia Gentileschi – Italian Baroque painter known for powerful works like Judith Slaying Holofernes.
  • Mary Cassatt – American Impressionist known for her paintings of mothers and children.

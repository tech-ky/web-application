Mid-Autumn Festival Light Display

This was one of the best experiences of the trip. We took many Polaroid pictures and spent the day exploring the area. The light show was absolutely mesmerizing.

Ice Cream Waffle

The portion was huge, shared among three of us! The strawberry and vanilla combination was so tasty. It was beautifully topped with flowers and osmanthus jelly. Definitely a must-try again!

Cafe Hopping

Though we slightly exceeded our budget, the food was absolutely worth it! A memorable experience at a cozy cafe.

Exploring a Bookstore

We spent hours browsing through a quaint little bookstore. I loved the cozy vibe and found some interesting reads.
